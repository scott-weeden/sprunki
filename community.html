Community Guidelines

To ensure a positive experience for all members, please follow these guidelines:

  • Be respectful and supportive of other community members
  • Give credit when using or remixing other creators' work
  • Keep content family-friendly and appropriate for all ages
  • Report any issues or violations to our moderation team

Get Involved

Ready to dive into the Sprunki community? Here's how you can get started:

  1. Create your Sprunki account and set up your profile
  2. Join our Discord server and introduce yourself
  3. Share your first mix in the "New Creations" forum thread
  4. Participate in the next community event or challenge
  5. Offer feedback and support to fellow Sprunki creators
